ST GEORGE'S AUTO FIT LIMITED - Required Images for /public folder
========================================================================

Please add the following images to your /public folder for the website to display properly:

ROOT FOLDER (/public):
- logo.png → Company logo
- hero.jpg → Hero background image (car wash or auto service photo)
- about.jpg → Image of staff or garage interior

SERVICES FOLDER (/public/services/):
- wash.jpg → Executive car wash service
- repair.jpg → Mechanical repairs
- diagnostic.jpg → Diagnostic services
- towing.jpg → Vehicle towing service
- alignment.jpg → Wheel alignment service
- detailing.jpg → Interior detailing service

Note: The website will work without these images, but placeholders will show until you add your actual photos.
